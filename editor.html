Concept

This project was a group assignment completed during high school, where we were tasked with producing a school promotional video using a stop-motion approach. My role focused on developing the creative concept, structuring the video narrative, and handling the full editing process from start to finish.

I designed the visual direction around a pixel game theme, combining stop-motion elements with digital editing techniques to create a distinctive and engaging style. The project aimed to present the school environment in a more creative and interactive way through a game-inspired visual experience.
