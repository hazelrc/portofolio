Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Hazel, a creative-tech enthusiast with experience in coding, 3D modeling, and video editing. In coding, I work with HTML/CSS for frontend, Java and Python for object-oriented programming, and Arduino for small systems like fingerprint locks, pin pads, servos, and sensors. I’ve built login pages, Arduino prototypes, and helped friends with simple websites. In 3D modeling, I use Blender to design rooms, create objects, and set up cinematic camera movement to bring scenes to life. In video editing, I use Adobe After Effects to create AMVs, cinematic sequences, and gaming edits that focus on rhythm and visual impact.
